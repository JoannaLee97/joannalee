About the Band

Weston Estate is a dynamic indie-alternative band known for their driving rhythms, captivating melodies, and electric live performances. Formed in a cramped garage by a group of lifelong friends, they quickly outgrew their local scene to capture the attention of listeners nationwide.

Drawing inspiration from classic rock roots and blending it with modern synth textures, Weston Estate has carved out a distinct sonic identity. With their highly anticipated upcoming releases and a rapidly growing fanbase, the band continues to push creative boundaries and redefine their genre.
